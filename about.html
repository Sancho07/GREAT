jeremyMusicWorld is a music company that sells innovative electronic instruments and accessories, help artists record their songs, album, or project, help artist promote their project and offer liveband and DJ services . The company was founded in 2022 by six friends, sampson, Mo dean, prosper, great, marvelous, and david kay, who were passionate about music and excellence.

Sampson, Mo dean, prosper, great, marvelous, and david kay were members of a garage band, called triumph minstrels. They loved to experiment with different sounds and genres using various gadgets and devices. They often modified their instruments adding sensors, light and effects.

OUR SERVICES

SALES OF MUSICAL EQUIPMENTS

Here at jeremyMusicWorld we provide sales of musical instruments to the community. We separate ourselves from our direct competitors by paying intense personal attention to our customers, and educating our clients about the products they need or desire before, during, and after the sale. We offer creative programs to reward customer loyalty, and we provide on-site repair services for the items we sell. We consider our staff our partners and insist they prosper equitably with the growth and success of the company.
